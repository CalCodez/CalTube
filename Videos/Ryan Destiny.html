Ryan Destiny

"How Many"




Ryan Destiny has established herself as a multi-hyphenate performer thanks to her start turns on television and her own records. In fact, she very recently reminded us of this when she released her latest single "How Many." However, she truly smolders when she's on the screen. The star capitalizes on this in the video for the cut.

Just like with her song "Do You," the clip for "How Many" is giving us major Aaliyah vibes. It begins with her walking into an empty warehouse in an ice-blue leather bralette with matching pants and shawl. She then steps into a mirrored box that lights up for a performance we won't soon forget.

The song's bass thuds and her black tresses (with ice blue highlights, of course) flow in the wind as she dances solo to the song's rhythm. From there on, she serves up sass and attitude as she lets a no-good man know that his luck has run out and she won't be the next one on his list to be played. Though her choreography isn't necessarily complex (save a backbend moment that might give Ciara a run for her money), she sells it with a quiet ferocity and sexiness that we haven't seen in quite some time.

We're loving the late '90s/early 2000s feel that Ryan Destiny is channeling here and would love to see and hear what she could do with a full-length project. Watch her do her thing in "How Many" when you press play below.
